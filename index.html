Research interests

My research interest is discovery breeding in plant species. My current work focuses on 1) dissecting yield components and phenological traits contributing to biomass yield of grass species, 2) investigating population and evolutionary genetics in perennial grasses, 3) genome wide association and genomic selection study in grasses species, and 4) evaluating prairie cordgrass (Spartina pectinata Link) as a bioenergy crop with ecological benefits.
